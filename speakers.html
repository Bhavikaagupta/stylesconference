Aaron Irizarry


DESIGNING A CULTURE OF DESIGN


Workplace culture does not start with beanbags, foosball tables, or a beer fridge,
and it does not end with neckties, PCs, or big corporations. It is the unwritten rules,
behavior, beliefs, and the motivations that enable good work to get done, or it is
what stifles a workforce. For design to be most effective and for designers to feel
valued, we need to work in a culture that embraces design and allows it to succeed.
In Aarons session he will explore how to recognize the traits of organizations that
*get* design, both large and small. He will share what those teams, departments,
and companies have that others do not, and more importantly, how to begin to
change your own workplace culture. Once you have worked within a culture of
design it is almost impossible to imagine yourself anywhere else.

ABOUT AARON


Aaron Irizarry is a Senior Product Designer for Nasdaq OMX, a lover of heavy metal,

AJ Self


(YOU SHOULD BE) TESTING YOUR JAVASCRIPT


JavaScript applications frequently utilize battle-tested libraries like jQuery,
AngularJS, Backbone.js and more, but how can we be sure that our code is ready
for production? This talk will share tips on how writing tests can be written easily
and quickly and how to remove buggy code through testing.


ABOUT AJ Self

Arman Ghosh


DESIGNING DEALS: HOW GOOD DESIGN DRIVES SALES


Perception influences decisions, especially when it comes to selling products and services.
Learn why before you even start sales conversations; good, thoughtful design
and presentation will define you and your ability to close deals.
